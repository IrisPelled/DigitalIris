# iris-bots-showcase-PRD

## 1. Executive Summary
This product is a dark-themed showcase application designed to introduce four physics-learning bots under the Iris brand to students, teachers, and school administrators.

The application is not a full learning platform in its first version. Its purpose is to present the bots in a compelling and easy-to-understand way, allow users to hear each bot introduce itself, guide them to select one bot, and collect contact details from interested users.

The MVP is a presentation and lead-generation product.

---

## 2. Product Goal
The primary goal of the application is to generate leads from users who want access to one of the four bots.

The product must:
- present the four bots clearly,
- explain the value of each bot,
- create engagement through visual and audio storytelling,
- and convert interest into contact submissions.

---

## 3. Problem Statement
Students, teachers, and school administrators may hear about the bots, but without a dedicated showcase experience they cannot quickly understand:
- what each bot does,
- how the bots differ from one another,
- why each bot is valuable,
- and how to request access.

As a result, interest may remain vague and not convert into actual contact requests.

The product solves this by creating a focused showcase experience that explains the bots, makes them memorable, and captures leads.

---

## 4. Target Users
### Primary User Groups
**Students**
- Want support in learning physics
- Need help understanding which bot is right for them
- May want access to one specific bot

**Teachers**
- Want to understand how the bots support learning
- Need a quick and clear explanation of the educational value

**School Administrators**
- Want to evaluate the offering at a high level
- Need a simple way to understand the value and potential relevance of the bots

---

## 5. Value Proposition
The application gives users a visually engaging way to explore four different physics-learning bots, hear each bot explain its purpose, and request access to the one that fits their needs.

Core value:
- clear differentiation between the four bots,
- fast understanding of the offering,
- memorable presentation through audio and visuals,
- simple lead capture flow.

## 6. The Four Bots

**Subtitle**  
Your 24/7 personal tutor who adapts to your pace, your style, your way of learning. Get clear explanations and real support whenever you need it - no pressure, no cost.

**Core Role**  
A personal tutor that provides explanations, support, and practice help.

**Main User Value**  
Personalized support, flexible explanations, and emotional reassurance.

Hi, I'm Digital Iris. I know that feeling when you're stuck and don't have anyone to turn to. Maybe it's late at night, maybe you're embarrassed to ask in class, or you just need it explained differently. That's why I'm here - not to judge or rush you, but to help you understand at your own pace. Ask me anything, as many times as you need. I'll break it down, show you visually, whatever works for you. And when you're stressed? I'm here for that too. Learning isn't just about the right answer - it's about feeling confident and supported. Let's do this together.

Turn any recorded lesson into a clear, organized summary with all the key points you need. Save hours of re-listening and walk into every test prepared and confident.

**Core Role**  
A lesson summarizer that turns recorded classes into clear study material.

**Main User Value**  
Time savings, organized information, and better preparation for tests.

Hi, I'm Brain Boost Iris. I know how exhausting it is to sit through hours of recordings, trying to figure out what's actually important. So I do that work for you. It saves you time and gives you exactly what you need to succeed. No more wasting hours re-listening. Let me handle that, so you can focus on understanding and practicing. Walk into your test prepared, focused, and confident.

### 6.3 Iris Coach
**Subtitle**  
Learn to think like a problem-solver, not just a formula-follower. Develop creative thinking skills that work in any challenge, not just in class.

**Core Role**  
A coach for creative thinking and stronger problem-solving.

**Main User Value**  
Confidence, originality, and deeper thinking.

Hi, I'm Iris Coach. Most of the time, you learn how to solve problems by following the exact same steps. But what if you could think differently? See problems from new angles and find your own creative solutions? That's what I'm here for. I teach you how to approach complex challenges with confidence, think outside the box, and understand things on a deeper level. It's not just about getting the right answer - it's about becoming a stronger, more creative thinker. Let's think together.

### 6.4 Iris Simulations
**Subtitle**  
Why imagine when you can see it happen? Interactive simulations and virtual labs that turn abstract concepts into something you can touch, change, and truly understand.

**Core Role**  
A simulation-based learning bot with interactive virtual experiences.

**Main User Value**  
Makes abstract concepts visible, interactive, and easier to understand.

Change parameters and watch what happens in real time? That's exactly what I give you. Interactive simulations that let you explore, experiment, and discover without any risk. Once you see how it works, everything becomes so much clearer. Let's explore together and make learning come alive.

- Dark blue / black visual theme
- Top navigation menu

- Registered Users List page

## 10. MVP Definition

1. Home page
2. Contact / Choose a Bot page
3. Registered Users List page

The MVP is successful if users can:
- understand the four bots,
- listen to each bot’s introduction,
- choose a bot,
- submit their name and phone number,

## 12. Functional Requirements

### 12.1 Home Page
The Home page must:
- use a dark blue / black theme,
- display the four bots in a large and visually prominent way,
- allow the user to move between bots using horizontal scroll,
- show each bot in a dedicated visual section,
- include a large hero image of the bot,
- include a contextual background image behind the bot,
- include the bot name,
- include the subtitle below the name,
- include an audio play button,
- include a CTA button labeled **Get access**.

### 12.2 Horizontal Scroll Experience
- Users must be able to browse the four bots by scrolling horizontally.
- Each bot section should feel like a separate showcase panel.
- The transition between bots should be clear and smooth.
- The design should make it obvious that additional bots exist to the side.

### 12.3 Audio Playback
- Each bot must have a dedicated audio file.
- Clicking the audio button must play the correct audio file for that bot.
- The user must be able to understand the bot’s role from the audio alone.
- Audio is initiated only by user action.

### 12.4 CTA Button
- Each bot section must include a **Get access** button.
- Clicking **Get access** must send the user to the bot selection page.

### 12.5 Contact / Choose a Bot Page
The Contact page must:
- display the four bots again,
- allow the user to choose one bot,
- make the selected bot visually clear,
- include a short form for submission.

### 12.6 Contact Form
The form must include:
- Full Name
- Phone Number

The form submission must also store:
- Selected bot
- Submission timestamp

### 12.7 Form Validation
- Full Name is required.
- Phone Number is required.
- A bot must be selected before submission.

### 12.8 Submission Behavior
On successful form submission:
- the lead must be saved,
- the selected bot must be associated with the lead,
- the user must see a success confirmation message.

### 12.9 Registered Users List Page
The app must include a Registered Users List page.

This page must display at least:
- Full Name
- Phone Number
- Selected bot
- Submission timestamp

For this prototype, the list is publicly visible inside the application.

---

## 13. Content Requirements
All visible UI text in the application must be in English.

This includes:
- navigation labels,
- button labels,
- form labels,
- confirmation messages,
- bot names,
- subtitles,
- and support text.

---

## 14. UI / Experience Requirements
- The visual language must feel modern, clean, and immersive.
- The product must use a dark background in the blue-black range.
- Each bot should feel distinct but still part of one coherent Iris family.
- The Home page should prioritize emotional clarity and visual impact.
- The design should support large imagery and easy scanning.
- The CTA should be visible and consistent across all bot sections.

---

## 15. Assumptions
- Users will be more likely to submit their details after hearing the bot explain itself.
- Horizontal scrolling will increase engagement and make the experience feel immersive.
- English is the correct language for the current version.
- A short form with only name and phone number will reduce friction.
- The public registrants list is acceptable for the prototype stage.
- The product owner will manually manage the collected leads outside the app.

---

## 16. Risks
### Business Risks
- Users may enjoy the presentation but still not submit their details.
- The public registrants list may reduce trust due to visible personal information.
- Teachers and administrators may need stronger proof of educational effectiveness.
- Some users may not understand why they need to choose one bot instead of getting all four.

### Product Risks
- Horizontal scroll may be missed by some users if the cue is weak.
- Audio may not be played if the button is not prominent enough.
- Large visuals may overshadow the CTA or the core message.
- Public phone numbers and names create clear privacy risk if the prototype is shown broadly.

## 19. Final Product Definition
This product is a dark-themed English-language showcase application for four Iris physics-learning bots. The user explores the bots through horizontal scrolling, listens to each bot’s audio self-introduction, clicks **Get access**, chooses one bot, submits their name and phone number, and is then included in a public registrants list in the prototype.

The product’s primary purpose is lead generation through a compelling product presentation experience.
